INVITATION

ATRACT: A Trustworthy Robotic Autonomous System to Support Casualty Triage

ATRACT Project Engagement Workshop Date: Friday 23rd May 2025 Location: Room LDN017, Loughborough University London, Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, HereEast, Stratford, London, E20 3BS Time: 1000-1400, including buffet lunch

To Whom It May Concern,

We are pleased to invite you to the ATRACT Project Engagement Workshop 2025. The ATRACT project aims to develop a trustworthy autonomous robotic (drone) system to support battlefield triage. The project (https://atracts.github.io/) is funded by UKRI-EPSRC via National Security and Defence sandpit. Now that we have passed the halfway stage, we are eager to engage with stakeholders, experts, and interested parties across relevant fields. The workshop will provide an opportunity to: 1) Share insights into the project's progress and key developments, 2) Gather valuable feedback to refine and enhance our approach, and 3) Foster discussions on challenges, opportunities, and potential collaborations. It will be a ‘closed’ event with attendance by invitation only. Your expertise and input would be greatly valued. We will share the agenda in due course. Please let us know if you would be interested in participating (RSVP to Prof Ardhendu Behera, beheraa@edgehill.ac.uk). Further information will be circulated closer to the date. Yours faithfully, ATRACT Team

A preliminary outline of the programme:

1000-1015 Introduction to Project 1015-1040 Keynote 1 on robotics and autonomous systems 1040-1100 Keynote 2 on AI, autonomy and ethics 1100-1115 Coffee break 1115-1200 Breakout group 1200-1215 Plenary Discussion 1215-1300 Lunch 1300-1320 Keynote 3 (Robotics, AI and Defence Medicine) 1320-1340 Keynote 4 (Human Science) 1340-1500 Final Discussion and Networking
